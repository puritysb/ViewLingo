Privacy-First Design

ViewLingo processes all translations on your device. We never see, collect, store, or transmit your content.

Our Privacy Commitment

ViewLingo is built with privacy as a core principle. We believe that your personal content should remain personal. This privacy policy explains how ViewLingo handles information when you use our screen translation application.

Information We DON'T Collect

ViewLingo is designed to collect zero personal data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Translation Content: Any text you translate stays on your device
  • Screen Content: Screenshots are processed locally and never transmitted
  • Usage Analytics: We don't track how you use the app
  • Personal Information: No names, emails, or contact details
  • Device Information: No device identifiers or system information
  • Location Data: We don't access or store location information
  • Browsing History: We can't see what websites or applications you translate

How ViewLingo Works

100% On-Device Processing

ViewLingo uses Apple's built-in Translation framework, which processes everything locally on your Mac:

  • Screen content is captured using macOS ScreenCaptureKit
  • Text recognition happens via Apple's Vision framework
  • Translation is performed by Apple's on-device neural translation engine
  • Results are displayed directly in your viewfinder overlay

No Internet Required

ViewLingo works completely offline. The app never connects to the internet for translation purposes. All language models are part of macOS and run entirely on your device.

Permissions Required

Screen Recording Permission

ViewLingo requires screen recording permission to capture the content within your viewfinder. This permission:

  • Only captures the specific area you select with the viewfinder
  • Is processed immediately and never stored
  • Cannot be accessed by us or transmitted anywhere
  • Is required by macOS for any app that captures screen content

Data Storage

Local Preferences Only

ViewLingo stores only basic app preferences on your device, including:

  • Your language selection preferences
  • Viewfinder size and position settings
  • Display preferences (themes, overlay settings)
  • Feature toggle states (like Live Mode on/off)

These preferences are stored locally using macOS UserDefaults and never leave your device.

No Translation History

ViewLingo does not maintain a history of your translations. Each translation session is independent, and previous translations are not stored or remembered.

Third-Party Services

ViewLingo does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app is completely self-contained and does not communicate with external servers.

Children's Privacy

ViewLingo does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any data, there are no special considerations for children's privacy beyond this general protection.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since ViewLingo doesn't collect data, any changes would likely be clarifications or updates to reflect new features that maintain our privacy-first approach.

Security

Because ViewLingo processes everything on-device and doesn't transmit data, traditional security concerns about data breaches don't apply. Your content never leaves your Mac, making it inherently secure.

App Store Security

ViewLingo is distributed through the Mac App Store and runs in Apple's secure sandbox environment, providing additional protection against malicious behavior.
