üõ°Ô∏è How ViewLingo Protects Your Privacy

100% On-Device Processing

All translations are performed using Apple's Translation framework, which runs entirely on your Mac. Your text never leaves your device, and we have no servers to send it to even if we wanted to (which we don't).

No Internet Required

Once you've downloaded the necessary language packs from Apple, ViewLingo works completely offline. You can translate sensitive documents, private emails, or confidential information with complete peace of mind.

No Third-Party Services

We don't use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services. ViewLingo is just you, your Mac, and Apple's built-in translation engine.

üì∏ Screen Recording Permission

ViewLingo requires Screen Recording permission to capture the text you want to translate. Here's what you need to know:

  • This permission is used only to capture text within the viewfinder you create
  • We only process the area you explicitly select
  • Captured images are processed locally and immediately discarded
  • Nothing is saved, stored, or transmitted
Important: The Screen Recording permission is granted to ViewLingo by macOS, not to us as developers. We have no way to access your screen remotely.

üîê Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there's nothing to secure on our end. However, ViewLingo follows best practices:

  • All processing happens in your device's secure memory
  • No temporary files are created
  • No cache of translations is maintained
  • Each translation is isolated and immediately discarded after display
